How to play

The user controls the player square using the arrow keys, and place bombs using the space key.

  • The green square is the player.
  • Blue squares are destructible walls.
  • Red squares are bombs.
  • Yellow squares are fire.
  • Dark green squares are extra bomb power-ups.
  • Gray squares are indestructible walls.
  • The purple square is an enemy monster.

Bombs have a slight delay before they explode, the fire can blow up destructible walls. If the player touches the fire or the monster they lose. Initially the player can only place one bomb at a time, but as they collect power ups they can place more.
